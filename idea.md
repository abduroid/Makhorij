# Ideas

## Games
 ### 1. Find the word by hearing the sound
    - The game will play a sound and the player has to guess the word associated with that sound
 ### 2. Harakat's   
    - The game will play pronunciation of a word with missing harakats and the player has to fill in the correct harakats
